What is GCP?

The Clobal Certification Program (CCP) is an Industry-Academia initiative to certify professionals with high-skills towards Industry 4.0 qualifications and the primary focus of GCP is the Automation and Smart Manufacturing (ASM) but not limited to Agriculture, Automotive and Timber industries.

Students receive both theoretical and practical laboratory instructions through a combination of online learning resources, hands-on testing and debugging of robotics and automation systems. Areas of study include Lab-based, Technical-based, Competency-based and MOOC-based modules.

The CCP equips low-skilled employees with a wide range of modern hands-on experiences in industry to achieve a high-skilled status. It also certifications from Supervisors, covers Technicians, Executives, and Engineers up to Managerial level.

How does the GCP work?

The GCP ecosystem consists of 3 stakeholders brought together by the GCP Online Platform

The stakeholders are

Content Supply Partners
Academic entities or training ogranization with training content or robotics and automation. Content consists online and offline courses (courses taught in physical classes)
Advisory Panel
Panel of experts from both industry and academia who are responsible to verify and accreditate content supplied from the Content Supply Partners before uploaded into the GCP Online Platform
Market Partners
Consumers of the training courses consisting of industry workers and students in the robotics field. Market Partners access the courses through the GCP Online Platform
